Gender Nouns


Overview of Grammar topics:

  • Today through the conversation between Marsha and Don we understood a special kind of nouns called GENDER NOUNS which are related to people, animals, things and birds.

  • Gender Nouns are mainly of three types Feminine gender, Neuter gender and Masculine gender.

  • A particular animal can have a different name and its genders can have different names too. For example: The feminine gender of ‘cat’ is ‘queen’ and its masculine gender is ’tom’.
